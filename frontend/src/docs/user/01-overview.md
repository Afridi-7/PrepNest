# Welcome to PrepNest

PrepNest is your **all-in-one exam prep companion**. Practice MCQs, take full-length mock tests, and chat with an AI tutor that explains concepts in plain language — all in a single, focused workspace.

## What you can do

- **Browse curated content** by exam category, subject, and chapter.
- **Practice** — answer MCQs at your own pace with instant explanations.
- **Take mock tests** — timed, scored, and reviewed like the real thing.
- **Ask the AI Tutor** anything from a quick definition to a full step-by-step walkthrough.
- **Track progress** — XP, levels, streaks, and per-subject mastery on your dashboard.

## Why PrepNest?

- **Focused, distraction-free UI** — built for studying, not scrolling.
- **Adaptive practice** — questions scale to your level.
- **Honest feedback** — every wrong answer comes with an explanation, not just a red X.
- **Fast** — pages load instantly so your study flow stays unbroken.

## How to use these docs

Use the sidebar on the left to jump between sections. Press <kbd>Ctrl/⌘ + K</kbd> (or the search box) to find a topic by keyword. Each section is short and task-focused — read what you need, skip the rest.

> **Tip:** brand-new? Head straight to **Getting Started** to create your account and run through your first practice session in under 3 minutes.
