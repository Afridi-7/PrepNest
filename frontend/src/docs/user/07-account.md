# Account & Settings

## Updating your profile

Click your avatar in the top-right → **Profile** to update:

- Display name
- Email (a verification link is sent if you change it)
- Password
- Theme preference (Light, Dark, or System)

## Theme

PrepNest fully supports **light** and **dark** modes plus a **system** setting that follows your OS. Switch any time using the sun/moon toggle in the navbar — your choice is remembered.

## Privacy

We collect only what's needed to run the app: account info, your study history, and product analytics. Read the full **Privacy Policy** linked in the footer.

You can request a data export or account deletion any time by emailing support.

## Logging out

Click your avatar → **Log out**. On a shared device, always log out before walking away.

## Deleting your account

Profile → **Danger zone** → **Delete account**. This is irreversible — your sessions, attempts, and conversations are permanently removed.
