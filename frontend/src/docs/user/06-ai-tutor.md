# AI Tutor

The **AI Tutor** is a friendly, patient explainer that's always available. It's not a search engine — it's more like a study buddy who never gets tired of your questions.

## What you can ask

- **Concept explanations** — "Explain Newton's third law with a real-world example."
- **Worked solutions** — paste a question and ask "walk me through this step by step."
- **Comparisons** — "What's the difference between mitosis and meiosis?"
- **Quick checks** — "Is my reasoning here correct?"

## How conversations work

Each chat is a separate **conversation** that the tutor remembers. Useful when you're tackling one big topic over multiple sittings — the tutor will recall what you've already covered.

Start a new conversation any time from the **+ New chat** button.

## Tips for better answers

- **Be specific.** "Why is option C wrong?" beats "I'm confused."
- **Paste the full question.** The tutor can't see your screen.
- **Ask follow-ups.** "Can you simplify that?" or "Give me another example."
- **Tell it your level.** "Explain this for a high-school student" tunes the language.

## Limits

The AI tutor is great at explaining and reasoning, but it's not infallible. If something feels off:

- Cross-check important facts.
- Ask the tutor to **show its reasoning** so you can spot any errors.
- Use **Report** to flag a clearly wrong answer — it helps us improve.
