# Practice Mode

Practice is the heart of PrepNest. Bite-sized question sets, instant feedback, no time pressure.

## Starting a session

1. Open **USAT** → pick a category → pick a subject → pick a chapter.
2. Click **Practice**.
3. Answer questions one at a time.

## During a session

- **Pick an option**, then click **Submit**.
- See the **explanation** — read it even when you got it right; it often deepens the concept.
- Click **Next** to continue, or **End session** to stop early. Your progress saves automatically.

## Question quality controls

Every MCQ is reviewed before it goes live. If you spot a typo, ambiguous wording, or a wrong answer, use the **Report** button — we read every report.

## Pro tips

- **Don't rush.** Read the explanation for *every* question.
- **Mix subjects.** Spend 10 minutes per subject rather than 60 on one.
- **Use the AI Tutor** when an explanation doesn't click — paste the question and ask "explain why option B is wrong."
- **Short, frequent sessions** beat long marathons. Aim for 15–25 questions per sit-down.
