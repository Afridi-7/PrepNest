# Frequently Asked Questions

### Is PrepNest free?
You can sign up and use core practice features for free. Premium tiers may unlock advanced mock tests, longer AI tutor context, and analytics.

### Which exams do you cover?
We currently focus on **USAT** categories. New exams are added regularly — let us know what you'd like to see.

### Do I need internet?
Yes. PrepNest is a web app and the AI tutor runs in the cloud. We're exploring offline practice for the future.

### Can I use PrepNest on my phone?
Yes — the entire app is responsive. Open the website on mobile Safari/Chrome and add it to your home screen for an app-like experience.

### Is the AI tutor always right?
It's accurate most of the time, but it can occasionally make mistakes — especially on niche topics. Always double-check important facts.

### How is my data used?
Your study activity stays private to your account. We use anonymized aggregate stats to improve content; we never sell personal data.

### Can I cancel any time?
Yes. If you have a paid plan, cancel from **Profile → Billing**. You keep access until the end of the current period.

### How do I report a wrong answer or typo?
Use the **Report** button on any question or AI message. We review every report.

### My streak reset! Why?
Streaks reset if you skip a calendar day in your local timezone. Even one question keeps it alive — set a daily reminder if you tend to forget.

### I forgot my password.
Click **Forgot password** on the login page. We'll email you a reset link valid for 1 hour.
