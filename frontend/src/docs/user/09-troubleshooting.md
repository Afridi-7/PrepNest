# Troubleshooting

### I can't log in
- Double-check the email and password — passwords are case-sensitive.
- If you signed up recently, make sure you've **verified your email**.
- Try **Forgot password** to reset.
- If the page just spins, your network may be blocking our API. Try another network.

### Verification email never arrived
- Check spam/junk and "Promotions" folders.
- Make sure your email provider isn't blocking us.
- Use **Resend verification** on the login screen.
- If still nothing, contact support — we can manually verify.

### A page won't load / I see a blank screen
- Hard-refresh: <kbd>Ctrl/⌘ + Shift + R</kbd>.
- Clear site data for our domain in your browser settings.
- Disable aggressive ad blockers or strict privacy extensions for our site.

### The AI tutor stopped mid-answer
- Click **Regenerate** to retry. Long answers occasionally hit a length cap.
- Check your internet connection — streaming is sensitive to flaky networks.

### My mock test timer ran out unexpectedly
- The timer is server-driven, so closing the tab does **not** pause it. Plan around uninterrupted blocks.
- If a clear bug occurred, contact support with the test ID (in your history) and we'll review.

### My XP / streak looks wrong
- Stats update within a few seconds. Refresh the page.
- Streaks use your **local** timezone — travel across timezones can shift the cutoff.

### Dark mode looks broken on my screen
- Toggle off and on again from the navbar.
- Hard-refresh to pick up the latest styles.
- Report it with a screenshot — include your OS, browser, and what you expected.

### Still stuck?
Email **support@prepnest.app** with:
- Your account email
- A short description
- A screenshot if relevant

We typically reply within one business day.
