# Getting Started

Get up and running in under 3 minutes.

## 1. Create an account

1. Click **Sign Up** in the top-right.
2. Enter your name, email, and a strong password (8+ characters, mix of letters/numbers).
3. Open the verification email we send you and click the link.

> Didn't get the email? Check your spam folder, then use **Resend verification** on the login page.

## 2. Log in

Use the email and password you just created. We'll keep you signed in on this device — log out from the avatar menu when you're done on a shared computer.

## 3. Pick your exam

From the **USAT** page, pick the category you're preparing for (e.g. Engineering, Medical). You'll see all the subjects available — Biology, Physics, Mathematics, and so on.

## 4. Start your first practice

1. Click any subject card.
2. Pick a chapter.
3. Hit **Practice** — you'll get a question, four options, and a built-in explanation after each answer.

That's it. Your progress is saved automatically.

## 5. (Optional) Try the AI Tutor

Stuck on a concept? Open **AI Tutor** from the navbar and type a question in plain English:

> "Explain photosynthesis like I'm 12."
>
> "Why does my answer to question 4 feel wrong?"

The tutor remembers each conversation, so you can keep going where you left off.

## What's next?

- Take your first **Mock Test** to see where you stand.
- Watch your **XP** and **streak** grow on the Dashboard.
- Read the **Feature Guides** for tips on getting more out of each tool.
