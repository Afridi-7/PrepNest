# Dashboard

Your **Dashboard** is mission control — a single screen that shows where you are and what to do next.

## What's on it

- **Stat cards** — total questions answered, accuracy, mock-test attempts.
- **Reward Quest** — your current Level, XP progress, and rewards unlocked at upcoming levels.
- **Recent activity** — a feed of your last practice sessions and mock tests.
- **Subject performance** — bar chart showing which subjects you're crushing and which need more love.

## Levels & XP

You earn XP for:

- Answering practice questions correctly (more XP for harder ones).
- Completing mock tests (bonus XP for high scores).
- Maintaining a daily streak.

Each level unlocks a small reward — bragging rights and (occasionally) special content.

## Streaks

Open PrepNest and answer at least one question on consecutive days to build a streak. Streaks reset at midnight in your local timezone if you skip a day.

> **Tip:** even one question is enough to keep the streak alive on a busy day.

## Reading the chart

The bar chart shows attempts per subject. Tall bars = lots of practice; short bars = subjects to revisit. Tap a bar to jump to that subject.
